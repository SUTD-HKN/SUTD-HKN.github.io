What is HKN?

Eta Kappa Nu (HKN) or IEEE-HKN is the international honor society of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE).

Founded in 1904 as a honor society for electrical engineering, it merged with IEEE in 2010, expanding its membership eligibility to all IEEE fields of interest.

The Mu Epsilon chapter at Singapore University of Technology and Design was established on 15 December 2015. We conduct workshops and industry visits as a part of our outreach efforts to promote electrical and computer engineering.

Guiding Ideals

Scholarship

As an honour society, Eta Kappa Nu members are to carry with them the distinction of superior scholarship by doing their best in their academics.

Character

Eta Kappa Nu members are expected to have good character, have the willingness for hard work and be able to work with people.

Attitude

Eta Kappa Nu members must be naturally genial and willing to use their talents in service to their peers and community.
